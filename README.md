# company-management-system

Admin can:
Create users and assign/remove roles.
Create teams and add/remove users from teams.
View tickets and authorize absences requested by a Team Leader.


Team Leader can:
Clock in/out.
Add/remove users from their team.
View team members' work times.
Assign tasks to team members.
Schedule and plan meetings with team members.
Authorize absences for team members.
Request absences from the Admin.
Submit tickets to the Admin.


Worker can:
Clock in/out.
View their assigned tasks.
Manage task statuses.
Rate tasks.
View team meeting times and join meetings.
Request absences from the Team Leader.
Submit tickets to the Team Leader and Admin.
